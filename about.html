Wildflowers is a seasonal, creative magazine for girls ages 8-12 (although we think girls of all ages will enjoy it). From project tutorials to coloring pages to well-crafted stories to missionary biographies, Wildflowers inspires girls to live life creatively. Each season brings a new issue with a bonus Christmas issue. Give them truth, beauty, and goodness. Give them Wildflowers.
